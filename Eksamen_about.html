About SØR

SØR is a travel company offering tailormade backpacking journeys to South America. With over 10 years of travel experience across the continent, SØR knows the best places to go for extraordinary experiences. Either it's Colombia in the north, or Chile in the south, we'll guide you through the destinations' food, culture and nature.
