Chile

Positioned on the southern tip of South America, facing the South Pacific Ocean, Chile offers an exotic climate. From the Atacama desert in the North to the Andes Mountains, and the Torres del Paine National Prak in the south.

Bolivia

Forget about the coastline and discover places like Uyuni, the world's largest salt flat, Potosi, the world's highest city 4000 meters above sea level, or embak on a journey in the lush and mysterious Amazonas.

Peru

Peru offers explorers the joy of historical Machu Picchu, Pracas' dry deserts right by the sea, the food capital Lima and picture perfect beaches in Mancora. With a rich culture and history, dating all the way back to the Inca Empire, Peru keeps you longing back.

Colombia

North west of South America, facing the Caribbean Sea, Colombia offers it's visitors an exotic cultural experience. Either it's salsa, coffee or beautiful beaches, Colombia got you covered.
